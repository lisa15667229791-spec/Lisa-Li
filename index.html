About Me

I am a PhD student in Applied Linguistics with teaching and research interests in Second Language Acquisition, L2 writing, GenAI engagement, Task-based Language Teaching, Corpus Linguistics, and Chinese language education.

My work explores how emerging technologies, especially generative AI, can support Second language learning, writing development, learner engagement, and pedagogically meaningful classroom practices.

Research Interests

AI Feedback in L2 Writing

I examine how learners engage with generative AI feedback during writing revision and how this engagement differs across proficiency levels.

Task-Based Language Teaching

I am interested in how TBLT principles can guide meaningful task design in Chinese and English language classrooms.

Digital Multimodal Composing

My research also explores how learners use language, images, audio, and digital tools to construct meaning in multimodal composing tasks.

Corpus Linguistics

I use corpus-informed approaches to investigate learner language, natural language use, and data-driven learning materials.

Teaching

I have experience teaching Chinese as a Foreign Language and English structure and composition. My teaching emphasizes meaningful communication, learner reflection, collaborative learning, and technology-enhanced instruction.

  • Chinese as a Foreign Language
  • EFL Structure and Composition
  • Task-based classroom activities
  • AI-supported writing and revision

Current Projects

GenAI Feedback in Chinese Writing Revision

This project investigates how Chinese language learners use generative AI feedback to revise their writing, with attention to revision types, learner uptake, and proficiency-related differences.

GenAI-Assisted Digital Multimodal Composing

This study explores how learners engage with generative AI during repeated digital multimodal composing tasks in a Chinese language classroom.

Teacher–GenAI Collaborative Task Design

This project examines how a language teacher collaborates with generative AI to design task-based activities for beginner Chinese learners.
